Content

Home page

This page will explain the history of the San Lorenzo Club, how it was created, and the motives the founders had to create it. The page will also explain the different locations that the stadium had and why they had to move it. It will explain the various capacities the stadium had and the expansion. They also will find information about the costs of the tickets for the different matches and the explanation of the different sectors of the stadium along with the better places to enter. This page will call the attention with the simpleness of the design but the complete information. This page is intended to call the attention of all the fans and people that follow this soccer club. It is intended for all ages, but more centered in people between 15 and 45 years, just because they are used to computers and the internet.
